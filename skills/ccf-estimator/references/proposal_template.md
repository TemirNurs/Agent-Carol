```
TOTAL PROPOSAL AMOUNT    $XX,XXX

Date: [date]

Valid For: 30 Days
```

### Page 2: Scope of Work
Numbered sections, each with:
- Section title and price
- Paragraph describing work (paint systems, coat specs, areas)

### Page 3: Inclusions & Exclusions + Notes

**Inclusions** (bullet list with diamond markers):
- All labor, materials, equipment
- SW paint products per spec
- Specific paint systems called out
- Surface prep
- Masking/protection
- Night work if applicable
- Cleanup, supervision, QC
- Insurance (GL + WC)

**Exclusions** (bullet list with X markers):
- Wallcovering
- Electrostatic painting
- Items not in our scope
- Pressure washing (GC scope)
- Lead/asbestos abatement
- Scaffolding (price separately)

**Notes & Assumptions** (numbered list):
- Pricing basis
- Ceiling heights
- Work schedule
- Paint supplier
- Access requirements
- Validity period

### Page 4: Terms & Conditions + Signature Block

**Terms:**
1. Payment — Net 30
2. Change Orders — written, signed
3. Warranty — 1 year workmanship
4. Insurance — $1M/$2M GL, WC per NC
5. Schedule — start/end dates
6. Dispute Resolution — good faith, mediation in NC

**Signature block:**
Two-column: GC side | CCF side
Lines for: Signature, Printed Name, Title, Date
